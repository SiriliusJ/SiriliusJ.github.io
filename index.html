Biography

I’m an M.S. student at Washington University in St. Louis (ESE), supervised by Prof. Xudong Chen. My interests center on learning theory and its connections with optimization; recently I worked on combinatorial optimization and Hamiltonicity on graphons, and earlier explored generative models. I’m generally curious about applying mathematics across domains, especially in engineering. I am applying for Ph.D. programs for Fall 2026 entry.
